## <a name="to-copy-data-to-a-new-company-using-rapidstart-services"></a><span data-ttu-id="7d345-107">Per copiare dati in una nuova società utilizzando RapidStart Services</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7d345-107">To copy data to a new company using RapidStart Services</span></span>  
1. <span data-ttu-id="7d345-108">Apre la nuova società.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7d345-108">Open the new company.</span></span>  
2. <span data-ttu-id="7d345-109">Scegliere l'icona ![Cerca pagina o report](media/ui-search/search_small.png "icona Cerca pagina o report"), immettere **Foglio di lavoro configurazione**, quindi scegliere il collegamento correlato.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7d345-109">Choose the ![Search for Page or Report](media/ui-search/search_small.png "Search for Page or Report icon") icon, enter **Configuration Worksheet**, and then choose the related link.</span></span>  
3. <span data-ttu-id="7d345-110">Scegliere l'azione **Copia dati da società**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7d345-110">Choose the **Copy Data from Company**.</span></span>  
4. <span data-ttu-id="7d345-111">Nella finestra **Copia dati società**, scegliere una società da copiare dal campo **Copia da**, quindi scegliere **OK**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7d345-111">In the **Copy Company Data** window, choose a company to copy from in the **Copy From** field, and then choose the **OK** button.</span></span>
