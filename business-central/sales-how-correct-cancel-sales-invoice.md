## <a name="to-cancel-a-posted-sales-invoice"></a><span data-ttu-id="1eed5-129">Per annullare una fattura di vendita registrata</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="1eed5-129">To cancel a posted sales invoice</span></span>
