## <a name="to-correct-a-posted-sales-invoice"></a><span data-ttu-id="6b5e8-125">Per correggere una fattura di vendita registrata</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6b5e8-125">To correct a posted sales invoice</span></span>

    > [!NOTE]
