> [!NOTE]  
> <span data-ttu-id="56187-120">Una volta eseguita la connessione a [!INCLUDE[d365fin](includes/d365fin_md.md)], non verrà richiesto nuovamente di eseguire l'accesso.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="56187-120">Once you have successfully connected to [!INCLUDE[d365fin](includes/d365fin_md.md)], you will not be prompted again to sign in.</span></span>
