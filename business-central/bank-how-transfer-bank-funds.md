3. <span data-ttu-id="3f447-110">Nel campo **Tipo conto** selezionare **Conti C/C bancari**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3f447-110">In the **Account Type** field, select **Bank Account**.</span></span>
