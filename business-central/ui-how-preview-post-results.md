## <a name="to-preview-gl-entries-that-will-result-from-posting-a-purchase-invoice"></a><span data-ttu-id="04766-105">Per visualizzare in anteprima i movimenti C/G che deriveranno dalla registrazione di una fattura di acquisto</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="04766-105">To preview G/L entries that will result from posting a purchase invoice</span></span>
1. <span data-ttu-id="04766-106">Scegliere l'icona ![Cerca pagina o report](media/ui-search/search_small.png "icona Cerca pagina o report"), immettere **Fatture di acquisto**, quindi scegliere il collegamento correlato.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="04766-106">Choose the ![Search for Page or Report](media/ui-search/search_small.png "Search for Page or Report icon") icon, enter **Purchase Invoices**, and then choose the related link.</span></span>
2. <span data-ttu-id="04766-107">Creare una fattura di acquisto.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="04766-107">Create a purchase invoice.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="04766-108">Per ulteriori informazioni, vedere [Registrare gli acquisti](purchasing-how-record-purchases.md).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="04766-108">For more information, see [Record Purchases](purchasing-how-record-purchases.md).</span></span>
